About Us

Where Imagination Meets the Ceiling

At Gypston, we believe that every ceiling holds the potential to elevate a space into something truly extraordinary. With a passion for innovation and a dedication to excellence, we specialize in crafting stunning false ceiling solutions that reflect the style, functionality, and aspirations of our clients. Our skilled team of designers, planners, and ceiling experts work closely with each client to understand their vision and transform it into elegant, high-quality overhead designs. Whether its a cozy home, a dynamic commercial space, or a luxury hospitality interior, we approach every project with creativity, precision, and professionalism.

What sets Gypston apart is our attention to detail and unwavering commitment to quality. From modern gypsum designs and wooden panel accents to lighting integration and acoustic control, every element is meticulously planned to deliver a seamless and captivating ceiling experience. Our mission is to create ceilings that are not only visually striking but also enhance the comfort and beauty of the spaces beneath them.

Gypston Founding

Gypston was founded by Gayasuddin, a visionary in the interior finishing industry with a deep passion for transforming ceilings into statement pieces. With years of experience in modern ceiling design and a commitment to quality craftsmanship, Gypston was established to redefine how we look aboveone ceiling at a time.

Completing The First Project

The company completes its first major false ceiling installationa premium residential project featuring custom gypsum designs and integrated lighting. This milestone set the tone for Gypstons commitment to quality, creativity, and customer satisfaction in every ceiling we craft.

Residential Projects

In the residential sector, Gypston collaborates closely with homeowners to bring their ceiling design dreams to life. By understanding their style, preferences, and daily living needs, we craft customized false ceiling solutions that enhance both aesthetics and comfort in every home.
